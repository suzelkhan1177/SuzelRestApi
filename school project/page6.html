Commerce

Commerce is based on the study of trade, finance, business activities, and the economy. If you enjoy dealing with numbers, analyzing data, and learning about trade, choosing commerce as a stream would be a good match for you.There are a wide variety of career options you can pursue in the field of commerce. Even if you are not a fan of mathematics, don’t worry, mathematics is not a compulsory subject in commerce and you can choose any of the variety of optional subjects available.

Accountancy

In any business or corporation, it’s important to analyze the economic success of the endeavour. The field of Accountancy or Accounting involves the measurement, processing and communication of the financial data of a particular business, corporation or other economic entity. Accountancy measures the results of the economic activities of an organization, communicating it to users such as investors, creditors, regulators and management
