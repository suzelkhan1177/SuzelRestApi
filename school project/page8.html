Career Planning

  1. Achievement: Doing work that yields results
  2. Recognition: Receiving attention for your work
  3. Helping Others: Providing assistance to individuals or groups
  4. Job Security: Enjoying a high probability that you will remain employed
  5. Helping Society: Contributing to the betterment of the world
  6. Challenge: Performing tasks that are difficult or new to you
  7. Influence: Having the ability to affect people's opinions and ideas
  8. Artistic Expression: Expressing one's artistic talents
  9. Leadership: Supervising/managing others
  10. Compensation: Receiving adequate pay
